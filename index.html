Faculty Research Talk

The Faculty Research Talks series is a colloquium that provides faculty Staff an opportunity to present their current scholarly activities to their colleagues, in a stimulating, collegial, and informal environment. The research can be at any stage or level, including ideas and concepts for projects, updates on work and analyses in progress, or a conference presentation.
